Overview

This section provides the details of the Speed Sensor circuit, including its components, assembly instructions, and functionality.

Components

  • Speed Sensor

  • Bread Board

  • T-Extension Board

  • 7x Jumper Cables

  • Green LED Light Bulb

  • Red LED Light Bulb

  • 2x 220Î© Resistors

  • Power source

Assembly Instructions

  1. Ground (GND): Connect the GND pin on the speed sensor to the ground rail on the breadboard.

  2. VCC: Connect the VCC pin on the speed sensor to the 5V pin on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header via the breadboard power rail.

  3. Digital Output (D0): Connect the D0 pin on the speed sensor to GPIO 17 (Pin 11) on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header.

  4. Connect the longer leg (anode) of the red LED to a row on the breadboard.

  5. Place a 220-ohm resistor between the shorter leg (cathode) of the red LED and the ground rail on the breadboard.

  6. Use a jumper wire to connect the same row as the anode of the red LED to GPIO 27 (Pin 13) on the Raspberry Pi.

  7. Similarly, place the green LED on the breadboard.

  8. Connect the longer leg (anode) to a free row.

  9. Place another 220-ohm resistor between the cathode of the green LED and ground.

  10. Use a jumper wire to connect the anode of the green LED to GPIO 22 (Pin 15).

  11. Connect the ground rail on the breadboard to a ground pin (GND) on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header (e.g., Pin 6).

  12. Connect the power rail on the breadboard to a 5V pin on the Raspberry Pi GPIO header (e.g., Pin 2).

Functionality
Testing the Circuit:

Use the below command to test the speed sensor.

$curl http://localhost:8080/speedSensor/enable

  • /enable - Initializes the Speed Sensor

$curl http://localhost:8080/speedSensor/speed/rpm

  • /speed/rpm - Returns the speed in RPM

$curl http://localhost:8080/speedSensor/speed/disable

  • /disable - Disables the Speed Sensor

Troubleshooting

Verify that all connections are correct and that the order of components in a row are correct.

YAML Configuration

The speed sensor as it appears in the application.yml:

digital-input:
  speed-sensor:
    name: Speed Sensor
    address: 17
    pulses-per-revolution: 20
    provider: pigpio-digital-input
    debounce: 500
